Our Story
Chicken Stop is fortunate to have first opened its doors in 2021 amidst a global pandemic. We are extremely grateful for our real-life Superheroes, our frontline workers!
Our passion for grilling and our chicken’s signature family citrus recipe dates back 3-4 generations originating from South America, known for its BBQ grill or “asado”.
